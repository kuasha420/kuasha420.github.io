Executive Overview

Motion Mechanics introduces FlexTime™, a modern framework for high-quality, sustainable care. Our system integrates five core pillars:

Multi-Shift Operations

Structured shifts (Z, A, B, C), including the Night Relief Lab™, to expand access for all patient schedules.

Diversified Session Model

A four-tier session model (Foundation, Standard, Premium, Express) aligned with clinical value and patient needs.

Scalable Tiered Staffing

An interactive 5-tier staffing model that balances payroll costs with service capacity and future growth.

AccessCare™ Program

A compassionate, subsidized program to ensure equitable care is woven into our sustainable model.

Data-Driven Financials

Interactive models for staffing tiers, occupancy rates, and launch projections provide a clear, dynamic financial backbone for decision-making.

AccessCare™ Program

A structured, sustainable system for ensuring care accessibility is a core pillar of our mission.

Daily Subsidized Slots

8 sessions/day (~9% of total capacity) are reserved for the AccessCare™ program.

Discount Value

A 30% discount is applied to Standard Sessions for all eligible beneficiaries.

Eligible Beneficiaries

  • Students & Teachers
  • Single-parent households
  • Chronic care patients (>12 sessions)
  • Community health referrals

Financial Impact

Represents ~1.3 million BDT/year in subsidized care. The business remains highly profitable at all modeled occupancy levels.

Purpose and Brand Value

Ensures equitable access to premium care, reinforces our social responsibility, and builds deep, lasting community trust and brand loyalty.

Strategic Advantages

Consistent Coverage: Predictable multi-shift operations ensure reliable patient care.

Expanded Access: Evening and future morning hours cater to professionals and students.

Premium Offerings: Differentiated session types (Premium, Express) increase perceived value.

Scalable Architecture: Tiered staffing with floaters allows for flexible, demand-based growth.

Sustainable Revenue: Model is profitable even at low-to-mid occupancy rates.

Community Trust: The AccessCare™ program builds a strong, positive public-facing brand.
