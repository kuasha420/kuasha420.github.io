How to Create & Verify an Email Alias

Aliases allow you to send and receive messages on behalf of a planet, department, or shared inbox (like mm@purrfecthq.com or career@purrfecthq.com) — while still using your own login. They keep communication unified, intentional, and transparent.

1️⃣ Go to Gmail Settings

Click the ⚙️ icon → See all settings → Accounts and Import

2️⃣ Add the Alias

Under “Send mail as,” click “Add another email address”

  • Name: Heavenly Council of Fur (or relevant planet name)
  • Email: hcof@purrfecthq.com
  • Check “Treat as an alias” → Next → Send Verification

3️⃣ Verify the Alias

A confirmation email will arrive in the shared inbox (e.g., hcof@purrfecthq.com).
Click the verification link to confirm your authority to send as that alias.

4️⃣ Compose with Alias

When writing an email, open the From dropdown and select your alias address.
Example: career@purrfecthq.com for recruitment-related messages.

5️⃣ (Optional) Set a Default Alias

If you mostly represent a planet or department, set that alias as your default sending address.
