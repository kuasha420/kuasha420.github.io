The Purrfect DNA

Our values are the foundational code of our universe. They guide our actions, our decisions, and our interactions, ensuring we build a world that is both innovative and kind. This isn't a list of ideals; it's a balanced system we live by.

Care Over Profit

We prioritize the well-being of our people and communities. Profit is a tool for stability, not the ultimate goal.

Trust Over Control

Clarity Over Complexity

Play Over Prescription

The Law of Forty Hours

This isn't a perk or a productivity hack—it's a core constitutional right. We reject the false heroism of exhaustion and believe that rest is not an absence of work, but a vital part of it. This section explores the principles and practices of our most sacred law.

Rest is Part of the Work

The Purrfect Universe exists to prove that great things can be built without breaking the beings who build them. The 40-hour max week is a shield. It protects creativity, family, and the right to simply exist without guilt.

We believe rested minds make fewer mistakes, generate better ideas, and build kinder worlds. We refuse to glorify burnout as commitment.

Rules of Balance

  • 40-Hour Maximum: No full-time being may be expected or scheduled to work beyond 40 hours per week.
  • Voluntary Overtime: Any work beyond 40 hours must be voluntary, temporary, and compensated with rest or bonus.
  • No Hero Points: Delivering miracles while neglecting life is not valorized. Sustainable excellence is the goal.
  • Transparency: All planets must maintain transparent timesheets to ensure adherence and accountability.

Protecting Our Time

  • Emergency Override: Temporary exceedance is allowed only in genuine planetary crises, with Council review and immediate compensatory rest.
  • Quarterly Audits: HR conducts work-hour audits. Violations trigger restorative reviews, not punishments. The goal is healing the system.
  • Annual Reflection: Every year, each planet must report on how they protected their people's time and made space for life.

The Constitution

This is our living promise—a charter of care, creativity, and accountability. It is the code that defines our shared values and sets the standards for our behavior. Explore the articles that form the foundation of our universe. Click on any article to expand and read its contents.

We are a federation of planets—diverse in form, united in ethos. Purrfect Universe (PU) is the parent entity, and each planet (subsidiary) operates autonomously but aligns with this constitution. Our purpose is to create ethical, sustainable, and accessible innovations that uplift communities.

This Article codifies our core values: Care over Profit, Trust over Control, Clarity over Complexity, and Play over Prescription. It also enshrines the 40-Hour Maximum Work Week as a fundamental right, rejecting burnout as a measure of dedication.

No individual shall be subjected to harassment, discrimination, or retribution. This Shield guarantees physical, psychological, and emotional safety. It is a promise of safe passage for truth, with formal processes for raising concerns without fear. Any attack on one member is regarded as an attack on the Universe.

Violations are investigated by HR and the Council, with sanctions ranging from warnings to dismissal. Amendments require a 66% supermajority and a public Request for Comments (RFC). The constitution is reviewed annually for relevance, clarity, and fairness.
