What Are Purrfect Hustles?

High-intensity, short-orbit ventures — prototypes, pilots, or micro-startups that emerge from Universe members and are powered by shared resources.

Not every idea needs a planet. Some just need a spark and a little cosmic dust.

Purpose

  • Encourage experimentation and fearless creation

    Provide a low-risk environment to test bold new ideas.

  • Offer every member a sandbox for innovation

    Give everyone access to tools and support for building.

  • Build ventures that can evolve, merge, or conclude gracefully

    Focus on learning outcomes, not just permanent companies.

  • Keep the Universe alive with movement, curiosity, and learning

    Foster a dynamic culture of continuous creation and discovery.

How It Works

Spark

Orbit

60–90 days pilot or PoC phase

Decision

Scale, merge, or sunset with reflection
